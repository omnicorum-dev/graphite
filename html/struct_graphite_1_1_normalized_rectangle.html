Detailed Description

Axis-aligned rectangle that has been clipped to canvas bounds.

Used internally by draw routines to avoid per-pixel bounds checks. After a successful call to normalizeRectangle(), x1/y1/x2/y2 hold the clipped (safe) extents and ox1/oy1/ox2/oy2 hold the original, unclipped extents (useful for computing UV offsets).
